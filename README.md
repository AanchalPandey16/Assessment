## Key Patterns Observed

The platform has a ROAS of 0.34, showing low ad efficiency.

**Category performance**
- Efficient categories: Baby Care, Devices, Nutrition with high revenue per ad spend
- Inefficient categories: Makeup, Skincare with high sales but low ROAS

**Product trends**
- Some top revenue products have low ROAS, meaning high sales do not always mean ads are effective
- Some mid-revenue products have good ROAS, showing smarter ad targeting

**Daily volatility**
- Revenue changes a lot across days and categories
- Some days are revenue outliers, likely due to promotions or unusual demand

**Correlations**
- Category-level revenue is highly correlated (0.86), meaning categories move together, possibly due to campaigns or seasonal trends

## Recommendations

1. Reallocate ad spend by reducing budget for low-ROAS categories and increasing it for efficient categories
2. Promote high-ROAS mid-revenue products to focus marketing on products that perform efficiently
3. Check revenue outlier days to understand spikes or drops caused by promotions, events, or errors
4. Optimize product mix by bundling or cross-promoting underperforming products with top performers
5. Monitor daily volatility to track category-level daily revenue and ROAS and spot anomalies quickly
